# Adjectives: indefinite vs. definite

## Indefinite forms

Used with no article or with **en**/**ett**:

- **en grön kopp** — *a green cup*
- **ett grönt äpple** — *a green apple*

## Definite forms

Used with **den**/**det**/**de**, or in the plural:

- **den gröna koppen** — *the green cup*
- **det gröna äpplet** — *the green apple*
- **de gröna kopparna** — *the green cups*

> **Rule:** Indefinite singular uses gender-specific endings (`-Ø` for en-words, `-t` for ett-words). Definite and all plurals use `-a` uniformly.

## Color forms

| *brown* | **brun** | **brunt** | **bruna** |
| *black* | **svart** | **svart** | **svarta** |
| *grey* | **grå** | **grått** | **gråa** |
| *red* | **röd** | **rött** | **röda** |
| *blue* | **blå** | **blått** | **blåa** |
| *pink* | **rosa** | **rosa** | **rosa** |
